HiddenChat · Privacy Policy

Nothing you write leaves your phone.

Your conversations are processed by a model that runs entirely on your device. They are never sent to any server. Not ours, not anyone's. We have no servers that receive them.

We collect nothing.

No account, no sign-up, no email. We do not collect analytics, usage statistics, identifiers or crash reports. We cannot see who you are or what you use the app for.

Your chats.

They stay on your device and you can delete them at any time from Settings. Uninstalling the app deletes them too.

When the app does use the internet.

Only for two things, both optional and never involving your conversations: downloading the assistant you choose (a file, over HTTPS, from public repositories), and checking for an updated model list. Once downloaded, the app works with no connection at all.

Purchases.

Handled entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details. The app only stores, on your own device, whether a purchase was made.
