Quick answers

First run. The app has no assistant bundled, so on first launch it downloads the one you pick over the internet. Use Wi-Fi. Once it finishes, the app works fully offline.

Purchases. To restore a previous purchase on a new device, open Settings, then Purchases, then Restore purchase.

Privacy. Your conversations never leave your device. See our privacy policy.
